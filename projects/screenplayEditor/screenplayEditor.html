Love In Progress

Written by

John A. Mobley

EXT. DOG PARK - DAY

The film opens with a close-up of HENRY, a studly black lab, strolling in slow motion, looking actually cool. The park is alive with the sound of BARKING DOGS and the murmur of OWNERS. Cool music plays in the background.

CUT TO:

EXT. DOG PARK - CONTINUOUS

Cut to a medium shot, JOHN (turning 40, dreamer) is walking Henry in, sunglasses on, feeling themselves. They both look like they own the place.

CUT TO:

EXT. DOG PARK - CONTINUOUS

Cut to what they are looking at, TWO LOVELY LADIES smiling back at first, then bursting out laughing. John checks if his fly is down, then looks down at Henry, who is going to town on his own junk as dogs will sometimes do, embarrassing John.

JOHN

(feeling embarrassed) C'mon man!

FADE OUT.

INT. THE CARDINAL - WEDDING VENUE - DAY

The venue is alive with the sound of a STRING QUARTET and the murmur of GUESTS. At the altar, JOHN (turning 40, dreamer) stands, a proud smile on his face as he watches MEGAN (early 20s, the epitome of grace) glide down the aisle in a wedding dress.

VOICE (V.O.)

(muffled) Do you...

The voice grows louder, more insistent.

VOICE (V.O.)

(clearer) Do you...

CUT TO:

INT. THE CARDINAL - MAIN FLOOR - CONTINUOUS

A GUEST, MR. HARRIS (50s, distinguished), approaches JOHN holding a tray of hors d'oeuvres.

MR. HARRIS

(curious) What do you have there?

JOHN

Oh, these are... uh, petite gourmet... cheese... delights.

MR. HARRIS

(skeptical) Cheese puffs. Just say cheese puffs.

John nods, embarrassed, and quickly moves away.

CUT TO:

INT. THE CARDINAL - KITCHEN - CONTINUOUS

SAM (early 30s, sharp) is prepping another serving tray. JOHN enters, sneaking a peek through the window at MEGAN.

SAM

(not looking up) She's a little young for you, don't you think?

JOHN

(grinning) Age is just a number, right?

ANDREA

Do they like the cheese puffs?

SAM

No, but John likes Megan.

SETH

He's a guy. Even gay guys like Megan.

ANDREA

Hey! You never say gay guys like me!

SETH

That's 'cause they love you. You both like BLANK, like, a lot a lot.

ANDREA

Don't tell my employees that! What are they going to think?

SETH

That you boss them around with a dirty mouth?

JOHN

TMI folks. TMI. There's ladies present.

Everyone chuckles, including SAM, who shakes her head at the banter.

FADE OUT.

INT. THE CARDINAL - KITCHEN - CONTINUOUS

The kitchen is bustling with activity. SAM is still prepping the serving tray when ROBERT (mid 30s, desperate soon-to-be dad) bursts in, his eyes wild with urgency.

ROBERT

(frantic) I need salty, sweet, savory, and bitter— all at once, plus umami! It's for Courtney.

SAM

(raising an eyebrow) That's quite the order. Are we feeding her or conducting a taste experiment?

JOHN

(trying to help) How about some olives for salty, chocolate for sweet...

ANDREA

(interrupting) ...and for savory, those mini quiches.

SETH

(chiming in) Bitter? Uh, toss in some kale chips.

SAM

And for umami, a splash of soy sauce on the side?

ROBERT

(nodding frantically) Yes, yes, that's perfect! She'll love it. Or hate it. I don't know, but I need it now!

Everyone springs into action, grabbing the various items and assembling a plate that looks more like a painter's palette than a meal.

COURTNEY (mid 30s, amused)

What in the world is that?

ROBERT

(beaming) It's everything you wanted, all the flavors of the world!

COURTNEY

(laughing) I just wanted a pickle.

The group erupts in laughter, the tension breaking as they all share a moment of relief and camaraderie.

FADE OUT.
